Model & Training

• Bi-LSTM (2 layers, 256 hidden, drop-0.3)
• Training on 2 M time-steps per factory, FL round = 1 epoch
• Optimiser: Ranger QHAdam, lr 5 e-4
• Gradients encrypted via Paillier 2048-bit; ε = 1.0 DP noise
