The Cost of Late Vision

NGOs in Bangladesh spend up to 7 days for manual flood delineation while waters keep rising. Each day adds an estimated $1.8 M USD to logistics overrun and lost harvest.

Solution · Hawkeye Engine

A SegFormer-B2 transformer consumes Sentinel-1 SAR, Sentinel-2 optical, plus SRTM slope. Training masks come from immutable physics rules:

  1. VV < 0.2 dB → smooth water radar return
  2. NDWI > 0.1 → high surface moisture
  3. slope < 0.05 rad → plausible flood plain
